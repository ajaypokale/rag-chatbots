About Us

At Maplewood High, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and challenging environment for our students. Our rich history is built on a foundation of academic excellence, strong community values, and a commitment to innovation.

We believe in a holistic approach to education, preparing students not just for college, but for life. Our dedicated faculty and staff are passionate about guiding each student on their unique journey.
